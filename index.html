Chewy is one of the most consistently deal-friendly retailers for pet owners. Their Autoship program saves 5–10% on recurring orders automatically, and they regularly issue email-exclusive codes reaching 35–40% off for new subscribers. Promo codes shared across deal communities often work site-wide and can stack with Autoship for serious savings.

What we love

  • Autoship stacks with promo codes
  • Email list has exclusive, verified codes
  • Outstanding customer service & returns
  • Free 1–2 day shipping on $49+

Watch out for

  • Best codes are new-customer-only
  • Codes sometimes expire unannounced
  • No in-store option
🏷️ Best deal type: Email exclusives + Autoship 📅 Last verified: May 29, 2026

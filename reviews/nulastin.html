What Is NULASTIN?

NULASTIN is a prestige beauty brand specializing in lash, brow and scalp growth serums. Founded in 2016 and celebrating its 10th anniversary in 2026, the brand has built a loyal following on the strength of its clinically tested, keratin-binding formulas. Unlike many serums that sit on the surface of the hair follicle, NULASTIN's proprietary Elastapure® technology is designed to bind to keratin — the protein that makes up hair — to support existing growth and reduce breakage.

The brand's hero products are the Lash Enhancing Serum, Brow Enhancing Serum, Scalp Treatment, and their bundled Icon sets. They also offer a Solution Serum System that combines multiple active ingredients for comprehensive hair health.

What We Tested

  • Lash Enhancing Serum — applied nightly along the lash line for 8 weeks
  • Brow Enhancing Serum — applied to brows morning and night for 8 weeks
  • Scalp Treatment — applied to scalp 3x per week for 8 weeks

Results After 8 Weeks

Lashes

The most noticeable results came from the lash serum. By week 4 we observed reduced lash shedding. By week 8 lashes appeared visibly longer and fuller — particularly at the outer corners where lashes tend to thin first. The effect was subtle enough that it didn't look artificial, but noticeable enough that people asked if we were wearing falsies.

Brows

Brow results were slower to show but equally impressive. Sparse areas filled in noticeably over the 8 weeks. Particularly effective for overplucked brows looking to regain density. The serum has a clear, lightweight consistency and dries quickly with no tackiness.

Scalp

The scalp treatment showed the subtlest results of the three — less shedding when brushing hair and a healthier-looking scalp overall. Best for those concerned about thinning or breakage rather than dramatic growth acceleration.

Compared to RapidLash and GrandeLASH-MD (the two most common drugstore alternatives), NULASTIN sits in a higher price tier but delivers noticeably better results in our testing. The keratin-binding technology feels like a meaningful differentiator rather than a marketing claim.

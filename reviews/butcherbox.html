What Is ButcherBox?

ButcherBox is a meat delivery subscription service that ships curated boxes of 100% grass-fed and grass-finished beef, free-range organic chicken, heritage-breed pork, and wild-caught seafood directly to your door. The company partners with farms that prioritize humane animal treatment and sustainable practices — no antibiotics or added hormones.

Boxes ship monthly (or every 2 months), are packed with dry ice and arrive frozen. Members can choose from curated boxes or build their own custom box. Prices typically run $146–$270 per box depending on size and contents.

What We Ordered — 3 Months

  • Month 1: Custom Classic Box — NY strip steaks, ground beef, chicken breasts, salmon
  • Month 2: All-Beef Box — ribeyes, chuck roast, ground beef, short ribs
  • Month 3: Custom box with pork additions — pork tenderloin, bacon, sausage

Meat Quality — Honest Assessment

Beef

The grass-fed beef is noticeably better than standard supermarket beef — leaner, with a slightly more complex flavor that takes a little adjustment if you're used to grain-finished beef but is genuinely superior once you're accustomed to it. The NY strips and ribeyes were impressive. The ground beef is exceptional — we now refuse to go back to grocery store ground beef.

Chicken

Free-range organic chicken that tastes like chicken is supposed to. Less water-logged than conventional supermarket chicken, better texture when cooked. The chicken breasts are a consistent highlight of every box.

Salmon

Wild-caught Alaskan sockeye salmon — rich, deep color, excellent flavor. Worth adding to any box if seafood is in your diet.

Packaging and Delivery

Every box arrived solidly frozen with no leaks or thawed product across all three months. The insulated packaging is recyclable. Delivery was consistent and on schedule.

Our Verdict:

ButcherBox delivers on its promise — the meat quality is genuinely better than what you'll find at most supermarkets, and the convenience of having high-quality protein stocked in your freezer is hard to overstate. It's not cheap, but for households that eat meat regularly and care about quality and sourcing, it's excellent value for what you get. We've been subscribers for over a year and have no plans to cancel.
